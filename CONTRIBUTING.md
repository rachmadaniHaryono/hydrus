# Contributing

This repo is for the most part a solo project by one guy with an unusual setup. Although I appreciate the work, spontaneous public pulls became too much to keep up with, particularly as AI kicked into gear, so pull requests are limited to collaborators only for now. If you have a really simple, say, five-line bugfix on a fork, please feel free to hit me up and I will pull from my end.

Bug reports and feature requests are welcome. Everything hydev writes is WTFPL, completely open, so feel free to fork and do whatever you wish on your end. I would be interested to know how you get on.
